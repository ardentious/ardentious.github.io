Myths vs. Reality

Fact-checking the most common rumors regarding Rockstar's acquisitions and structure.

Rumor / Variant of Truth

"Rockstar East & Rockstar West"

Many fans believe there are two giant umbrella divisions directing the studios in the East and West hemispheres.

The Reality

These are defensive trademark filings and fan colloquialisms. No physical development divisions exist under these names. Corporate strategy is run from Rockstar NYC.

Verified Strategic Fact

The Forza Vehicle Connection

Rumors suggested Rockstar poached Forza Horizon's car modelers to build GTA vehicles.

The Truth: Dhruva Interactive

Rockstar acquired Dhruva Interactive for $7.9M in 2019. Dhruva had modeled vehicles for Forza Horizon 2/3 and Motorsport 5/6. They were merged into Rockstar India.

The Human Capital Breakdown

Rockstar's personnel is concentrated in three main hubs: India, the UK, and the USA. This geographical spread ensures a "follow-the-sun" development model where assets can be iterated on 24/7 across time zones.

Compartmentalization & Security

To prevent leaks, developers are divided into "cells," often working on isolated tasks like a single NPC behavior or street layout without access to the full game build.

The Unionization Push

In May 2026, the Rockstar Games Workers Union was formed by IWGB, representing employees across five major UK studios in response to RTO policies and dismissals.
